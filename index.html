Your Personalized Portrait Journey

Step 1: Initial Consultation

We start with a friendly conversation to understand your vision. Whether it's a
cherished family portrait or a professional piece, we'll discuss the style, size, and setting that fits your needs.

Step 2: Taking Photos or In-Person Sketching

You’ll provide high-quality photos, or we can arrange a live sitting for a more
personalized touch. Together, we'll choose the best reference to capture the
essence of your portrait

Step 3: First Drawing

Our first artistic step begins with a detailed charcoal sketch. This initial rendering
helps you see the layout and proportions of the portrait.

Step 4: Feedback and Final Sketch Approval

Review the sketch and let us know if adjustments are needed. We’ll refine the
design until it meets your expectations. Once approved, we move on to the
painting phase.

Step 5: Creating Your Masterpiece

The oil painting process begins! Depending on the size and complexity, it usually
takes 2–4 weeks. We focus on every detail to ensure your portrait is lifelike and
captivating.

Step 6: Safe Delivery

Once completed, your painting is carefully packaged and shipped to your
preferred address. We handle it with care to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

Step 7: Optional Framing

If you’d like, we can provide professional framing at market pricing, tailored to
complement your portrait and home décor.
By following this process, you’ll receive a one-of-a-kind, hand-painted oil portrait
that you and your loved ones will treasure for generations.
